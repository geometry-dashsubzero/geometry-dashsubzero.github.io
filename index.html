About Geometry Dash Subzero

Geometry Dash SubZero is a thrilling rhythm-based platformer spin-off from the well-known Geometry Dash series, featuring three progressively tougher levels. Control your speedy geometric cube to navigate, jump, and finish the challenging levels. This may require many attempts, patience, and more importantly, quick reflexes and right timing. Keep practicing and retrying, and you’ll finish the levels.

What is Geometry Dash Subzero

Geometry Dash Subzero was released in December 2017 as a mini-expansion to preview features coming in the main game’s update 2.2. As you probably know, RobTop Games is the developer behind Geometry Dash, and CrystalKeeper7 created this Scratch version.

There are only three fast-paced platforming levels in the game, named Press Start (the easiest), Nock Em, and Power Trip (the hardest), each synced to high-energy music. Like in the original game, there are no forgiving checkpoints in normal mode. That means a single failure will send you back to the start immediately.

How to Play Geometry Dash Subzero: Guides & Tips

The game is easy to pick up and control but incredibly hard to master due to its lightning-fast pace and relentless, tricky obstacles.

Jump over obstacles and stay in rhythm

Time your jumps precisely to safely avoid dangerous obstacles like spikes, traps, etc., scattered along the path. Hitting any hazards along your way will end your run immediately. Remember, there’s no place for even a single mistake here.

How to win the level

You only complete a level when your cube-shaped character survives the entire level without crashing. To be honest, this is a tough challenge that often takes players multiple tries to complete. So, if you can finish a level within one or a few attempts, you are one of the top players.

Use practice mode

If you find surviving the entire level from start to finish without crashing extremely challenging, the practice mode, which allows you to set checkpoints, can be a game-changer. Use this solution to train on tricky parts without restarting the entire level.

Game controls

  • Tap, left-mouse click, or press the space bar to jump.

You think you have top-notch timing and precision? Think again, since Geometry Dash Subzero will push them to the limit. Jump in and see if you can conquer all challenging levels within the game, especially the Power Trip.
